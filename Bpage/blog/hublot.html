The Hublot Revolution

Hublot has always been a brand that dares to be different. Since its founding in 1980, the Swiss watchmaker has redefined luxury with its bold designs, innovative materials, and a philosophy known as the "Art of Fusion." Hublot’s timepieces are a celebration of creativity, craftsmanship, and the courage to break the mold.

The Birth of a Legend

Hublot’s debut watch was the first to combine natural rubber with precious gold—a daring move that shocked the industry and set the stage for decades of innovation. This fusion of materials became Hublot’s signature, inspiring countless imitations but never equaled in originality or impact.

Innovation Without Limits

From the iconic Big Bang to the avant-garde MP collection, Hublot continues to push boundaries. The brand experiments with ceramics, carbon fiber, sapphire, and even magic gold—an exclusive scratch-resistant alloy. Each watch is a testament to Hublot’s relentless pursuit of perfection and its refusal to follow trends.

Luxury With a Bold Identity

Hublot watches are instantly recognizable for their bold cases, exposed screws, and sporty elegance. Worn by athletes, celebrities, and collectors, Hublot has become a symbol of status and individuality in the world of haute horlogerie.

Experience the Hublot Revolution

To own a Hublot is to embrace the spirit of innovation and individuality. Discover the collection and experience the revolution that continues to shape the future of luxury watchmaking.
